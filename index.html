About Me

Making peoples lives easier

My name is Cole Bishop, I'm a software engineer with 3 years of experience building high quality applications that offers an easy to pick up user experience. I got into this field for one reason, to make peoples lives better through software and whether its building games that allow people to just sit down and have fun or developing tools that save them hours of time, I love the entire process.

I have experience across the board, full stack web development, building Android mobile apps, working with game engines, handling relational databases, networking fundamentals, version control like Git, and dealing with any OS including Linux. All aspects of this field catch my interest which leads me to pick up skills and learn new things that make me a more well rounded developer that isnt limited to any specific niche.

Above all else, the bottom line is that I take pride in everything I do and that extends to my work. I like to know that any code I put into the world is as efficent and effective as possible. Outside of my interest in Software Engineering, my days consist of going on hikes, working out, and getting wrapped up in personal projects.
